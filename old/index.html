Planes, Trains, and Automobiles

Over the course of the 20th Century, how has our perception of time and space changed, and what has facilitated these changes?

Hello and welcome!

Here, I will discuss the rapid changes that took place from the 1950's to the end of the 20th century in science. I will focus on telecommunications, transportation, photo/video, and scientific theory. I will provide evidence that those changes had a huge impact on our perception of time and space, which impacted our art.
