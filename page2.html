TUNING

Before you can play anything, the uke must be in tune. Like really perfectly in tune. I found a great source on youtube, here.
You just adjust the tuners until you reach the same tone in the video.

If you are in the playing position (assuming you are right handed), The soprano ukulele is tuned to the following notes (looking from the top down):

  • --G------
  • --C------
  • --E------
  • --A------

With the ukulele in tune, it is time to get some chords down!
